Adidas and Manchester United: A Powerful Partnership

Adidas and Manchester United have forged a powerful partnership that extends beyond traditional kit sponsorship. This collaboration has led to a series of innovative initiatives, from cutting-edge kit designs for the men’s, academy, and women’s teams to exclusive clothing lines and a strong commitment to developing talent across all levels of the club.

Kit Supply and Innovation

Since the 2015/16 season, Adidas has been the official kit supplier for Manchester United. This partnership has seen the creation of iconic kits that blend tradition with modern design, featuring bold colorways, innovative materials, and cutting-edge technology.

Exclusive Clothing Lines

Beyond the pitch, Adidas and Manchester United have collaborated on several exclusive clothing lines that celebrate the club's rich history and cultural impact. Some notable collections include:

  • AdiColor: A vibrant collection inspired by Adidas' heritage.
  • LFSTLR: A.K.A Lifestyler, this collection aims to target a younger audience.
  • Stone Roses: A collaboration with the iconic British band.
  • George Best: Honoring the legendary Manchester United player.

A Unified Approach to the Beautiful Game

Adidas and Manchester United are committed to fostering talent and excellence across all levels of the club. This includes the men's, academy, and women's teams. By providing high-quality kits, training gear, and support, Adidas empowers players of all ages and genders to reach their full potential.

This long-standing partnership between Adidas and Manchester United continues to shape the future of football, both on and off the pitch.
